Before you embark on this journey with Amplify, it is imperative that you understand and agree to our Data Disclosure Agreement. (A more detailed version can be downloaded at the link above).


You may use Amplify only for Vanderbilt purposes. It is your responsibility to maintain the security of your account. Do not share your login credentials with anyone else. 


You may securely share most confidential Vanderbilt data up to data classification “Level 3” with Amplify, including most personal data and student data covered by FERPA. But you must not share data Vanderbilt considers “Level 4 CRITICAL,” which includes: Payment Card information, Student Accounts and Financial aid, Controlled Unclassified Information or Export controlled information, or non-public law enforcement records created by VUPD. Generally, if you have access to Level 4 information you are already aware of its sensitivity, but it is your responsibility to confirm the Data Classification Level of Vanderbilt data before you share it with Amplify. For more information, check out the Data Classification Guidelines or contact datagovernance@vanderbilt.edu. 


Your inputs to Amplify are held securely on Vanderbilt-controlled data storage. As with using any other Vanderbilt-provided technology asset, your activity may be logged or analyzed by Vanderbilt to enable us to secure, understand, and improve this service. See the Appropriate Use of Technology Assets Policy.
